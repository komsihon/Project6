F.A.Q

{% trans 'What is “Deploy” ? What do I get from Deploying ?' %}

{% blocktrans %} Deploy simply means create your own online platform. What you get is the Kakocase suite under the name of your company. From then, you can start adding contacts in your Cloud Customer Manager, and set all kinds of revivals. You can publish your products on the platform and get customers to visit and buy online. Your platform is immediately accessible with the link http://go.ikwen.com/yourcompany. You can further move to yourcompany.com or yourcompany.cm when you buy your own domain name. See how to buy and configure domain name to discover how to do it. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans 'What I am paying for exactly ?' %}

{% blocktrans %} When you deploy your Kakocase platform, you get many business-effective online tools including :
  • An e-commerce website with online payments.
  • An automated system to store your customer’s contacts and revive them by targeted Mail and SMS.
  • SMS and Email Packs to deliver SMS and mails to your customers.
  • A hosting package to get all that running.
  • Continuous Maintenance, Software updates, Data security, Backups and more...
{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans 'How do I pay ?' %}

{% blocktrans %} Once you deploy your platform, you immediately receive an invoice by mail. You can view the invoice and pay by MTN MoMo online. Once you have done the payment. Your service is activated to run for 1 year. You can print the generated for your accounting. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans 'Do I pay once for all ? What happens after the 1 year ?' %}

{% blocktrans %} Payment covers the first three months of service. For your service to continue after those 1 year, you will have to pay for renewal fees that are always much lower than setup fees that cover another 1 year. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans 'What if I don’t pay at the end of the period, are my data deleted ?' %}

{% blocktrans %} When the end of the period is approaching, you receive at least 3 reminders with the new invoice to pay. If you do not pay on time, you are given a consistent amount of extra time to do the payment. After that period, your platform is shutdown, but all your data are kept. Everything will be put back in place when you pay your invoice. Your data are completely deleted after 1 year of inactivity. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans 'Can I pay for a year ?' %}

{% blocktrans %} Yes you can. You are highly encouraged to pay for a year as you will benefit a discount. Switch to annual payment view to see how much it costs and get your platform for a year. {% endblocktrans %}
